Preparedness Tips: Drop, Cover, and Hold during tremors. Do not use elevators. Have an evacuation plan. Keep emergency kits ready. 2. Floods Cause: Heavy rainfall, dam failure, river overflow, poor drainage. Effects: Loss of life, damage to property, waterborne diseases. Preparedness Tips: Move to higher ground. Switch off electrical supply. Store clean drinking water. Avoid walking/driving in floodwaters. 3. Cyclones / Hurricanes Cause: Severe tropical storms with high-speed winds. Effects: Roof damage, uprooted trees, flooding, power failure. Preparedness Tips: Secure doors and windows. Store food and medicines. Stay indoors in safe rooms. Follow official weather alerts. 4. Fire Accidents Cause: Short circuits, gas leaks, unattended flames. Effects: Burns, suffocation, property loss. Preparedness Tips: Know the fire exits. Practice fire drills. Use fire extinguishers (PASS method: Pull, Aim, Squeeze, Sweep). Stop, Drop, and Roll if clothes catch fire. 5. Drought Cause: Prolonged shortage of rainfall. Effects: Crop failure, food shortage, migration. Preparedness Tips: Store water carefully. Promote rainwater harvesting. Practice water-saving habits. 6. Landslides Cause: Heavy rains, deforestation, earthquakes, unplanned construction. Effects: Road blockages, building damage, loss of lives. Preparedness Tips: Avoid construction on steep slopes. Plant trees to prevent soil erosion. Stay away from risky hilly areas during heavy rains. 7. Pandemics / Epidemics (like COVID-19) Cause: Spread of infectious diseases. Effects: Illness, school closures, economic loss. Preparedness Tips: Follow hygiene practices (handwashing, masks). Get vaccinated.
